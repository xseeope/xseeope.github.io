&E(S_{t}^{w_{k}^{s}}R_{t+1}^{w_{k}^{s}})=\lambda_{k}(\Pi^{s}) \\

&(w_{k}^{s})=\argmax_{\mathrm{orthon.}\{x_{k}\}_{k=1}^{K}}\sum_{k}E(S_{t}^{x_{k}}R_{t+1}^{x_{k}}) \\
